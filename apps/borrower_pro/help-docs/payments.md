# Making payments

This guide explains how to make repayments in the portal.

Payments are made by bank transfer to the company's designated bank account. After you submit your payment, the admin team reviews it before your repayment schedule is updated.

## How payment works

### 1. Open your loan payment page

Go to your loan and select the payment option for that loan.

### 2. Choose the amount to pay

You can usually:

- pay the suggested monthly instalment, or
- enter a custom amount.

Always check the amount carefully before making the transfer.

### 3. Review the bank account details

The payment page shows the bank account details you should transfer to, including:

- bank name,
- account name, and
- account number.

Only transfer to the bank account shown on the payment page for your loan.

### 4. Use the transfer reference

The payment page generates a transfer reference for you.

- Copy the reference shown on the page.
- Enter the same value into your bank app or online banking reference field.
- Keep the same reference when you submit the payment in the portal.

This helps the admin team match your bank transfer to your loan payment request.

### 5. Complete the bank transfer

Make the payment from your bank account using your banking app, online banking, or another bank transfer method supported by your bank.

### 6. Upload your payment receipt if available

You may upload a receipt after making the transfer.

This is optional, but it can help support your payment submission.

### 7. Submit the payment for review

After the transfer is made:

- confirm the payment in the portal,
- include the generated transfer reference, and
- attach the receipt if you have one.

Your payment is then sent for admin review.

## What happens next

- The admin team reviews the submitted payment.
- Once the payment is verified, your loan schedule is updated.
- If the payment is not yet verified, the schedule may still show the earlier balance or due status.

## Important notes

- Payments are currently made through the bank account details shown in the portal.
- Use the correct transfer reference so your payment can be identified more easily.
- If the bank account details are missing, contact the admin team before making payment.
- If you pay after the due date, late payment charges may apply according to your loan terms.
