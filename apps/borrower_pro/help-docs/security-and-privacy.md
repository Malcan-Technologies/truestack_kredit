# Security and privacy

This guide explains, at a high level, how your privacy and personal data are protected when you use the portal.

We aim to protect your information so it is used only for legitimate loan, identity verification, compliance, account, and servicing purposes.

## What information is protected

This includes information such as:

- your personal details,
- your account information,
- your e-KYC data,
- identity documents and verification images, and
- loan-related records and signed agreements.

## How your privacy is protected

Your information is handled with privacy and confidentiality in mind.

In general:

- access is limited to authorized personnel and approved service providers who need it for legitimate work,
- information is used only for relevant business, legal, servicing, verification, and compliance purposes,
- records are retained only as needed for those purposes and applicable requirements, and
- internal access and handling are intended to reduce unnecessary exposure of your personal data.

## Encryption and protection of your data

Sensitive information is protected using encryption and related safeguards.

This includes data associated with your account, e-KYC process, and identity documents.

The website is protected using SSL/TLS encryption to help secure data sent between your device and the portal.

Your data is stored in Malaysia, kept encrypted, and backed up to support security, resilience, and recovery.

## e-KYC and identity documents

Your e-KYC information and identity documents are collected to:

- verify your identity,
- support safe lending,
- meet compliance requirements,
- reduce fraud and impersonation risk, and
- support issuance of the digital signing certificate used for signing the loan agreement.

Because these records are highly sensitive, they are handled with additional care and should only be used for the relevant verification, compliance, and loan-related purposes.

## Passwords and account access

Your password and account access are important parts of your security.

To help protect your account:

- choose a strong password,
- keep your password private,
- do not share login details with other people, and
- contact the admin team promptly if you suspect unauthorized access.

Passwords are protected using secure handling practices and are not intended to be exposed in plain text through the normal use of the portal.

Your password is also protected so it is not stored or shown in plain text as part of normal portal operations.

## Why this matters for digital signing

After e-KYC is successfully completed, your verified identity can be used to support issuance of the digital signing certificate needed for PKI signing.

This helps tie the signature to your verified identity and helps protect the integrity and immutability of the signed loan agreement.
