# Why e-KYC is required

This guide explains why you need to complete e-KYC before you can move forward in the loan journey.

e-KYC is used to verify your identity so the company can lend safely, meet compliance requirements, and prepare your digital signing certificate for the loan agreement.

## Why the portal asks you to complete e-KYC

### 1. To confirm that you are really you

e-KYC helps confirm that the person applying for the loan and signing the agreement is the real borrower.

This reduces the risk of:

- impersonation,
- identity misuse,
- fraudulent applications, and
- unauthorized signing.

### 2. To support safe lending

Identity verification is an important part of responsible and secure lending.

When your identity is verified:

- the company can process your application with greater confidence,
- your loan records can be tied to the correct person, and
- important loan actions are less likely to be completed by the wrong person.

### 3. To meet compliance requirements

e-KYC also supports compliance obligations.

Identity verification helps the company maintain a proper borrower record and follow the required checks before moving you to signing and disbursement.

## Why e-KYC matters for digital signing

After e-KYC is completed successfully, your verified identity can be used to obtain the digital signing certificate needed for signing the loan agreement.

## What the digital signing certificate does

The digital signing certificate is used for PKI signing of the loan agreement.

In simple terms, this helps:

- link the signature to your verified identity,
- protect the agreement from unauthorized changes, and
- support the immutability of the signed loan agreement after execution.

This means the signed agreement is intended to remain trustworthy and tamper-evident after it has been signed.

## Why e-KYC must happen before signing

The certificate used for signing depends on verified identity information.

Because of that, you usually cannot proceed to the digital signing step until your e-KYC has been completed successfully.

## What this means for you

- Complete e-KYC as soon as it becomes available in your loan journey.
- Make sure the information and images you provide are clear and accurate.
- Once e-KYC is verified, you can continue toward certificate issuance and digital signing.

## Summary

You are asked to complete e-KYC because it helps verify your identity, supports safe lending, meets compliance needs, and enables issuance of the digital signing certificate used to protect the integrity of your signed loan agreement.
