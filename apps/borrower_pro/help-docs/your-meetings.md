# Your Meetings

The **Meetings** page lists attestation meeting activity across your loans in one place — you do not have to open each loan to see what needs attention.

## What you will see

- **Action needed** — for example, your lender proposed a new time, a proposal is waiting, or the meeting is finished and you must **accept** or **reject** to continue.
- **Upcoming** — scheduled or proposed times that are still in progress.
- **Past** — completed meetings and history (when available).

You can add events to your calendar (`.ics` download), copy the Meet link, open the full attestation step on a loan, or go to the scheduling page to **reschedule** when your flow allows it.

## After your lender ends the meeting

When the attestation meeting is marked complete, you must **accept** the terms to move on to e-KYC and signing, or **reject** the loan if you do not wish to continue. The same choice appears on the loan page and on this hub.

Times are shown in **Malaysia** local time, consistent with the rest of the loan portal. The list refreshes periodically while you stay on the page.
