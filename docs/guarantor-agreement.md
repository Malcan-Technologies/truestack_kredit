# ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION

1.1 In this Deed, unless the context otherwise requires:

- **"Default"** means any failure by the Principal Debtor to pay or perform any obligation under the Principal Agreement when due.
- **"Guaranteed Amount"** means all monies and liabilities whatsoever (whether actual or contingent, present or future) now or hereafter owing or payable by the Principal Debtor to the Creditor under or in connection with the Facilities, including principal, interest, profit, default interest, late payment charges, commissions, fees, and all legal and recovery costs on a full indemnity basis.
- **"Principal Agreement"** means the Facility Agreement, Loan Agreement, and all related security and supplemental documents entered into between the Creditor and the Principal Debtor.

1.2 Words importing the singular include the plural and vice versa.

1.3 This Deed shall take effect as a deed and shall not require consideration to be binding.

---

# ARTICLE 2: GUARANTEE

2.1 In consideration of the Creditor granting or continuing the Facilities, the Guarantor hereby **irrevocably and unconditionally guarantees** to the Creditor the due and punctual payment and performance by the Principal Debtor of the Guaranteed Amount.

2.2 The liability of the Guarantor under this Deed shall be as **principal debtor and not merely as surety**.

2.3 The Creditor shall be entitled to enforce this Deed immediately upon Default without first:

- Taking action against the Principal Debtor;
- Enforcing any other security;
- Making demand on any other guarantor.

2.4 A certificate in writing signed by an authorised officer of the Creditor stating the amount due shall be **prima facie evidence** of the indebtedness for all purposes.

---

# ARTICLE 3: CONTINUING GUARANTEE

3.1 This is a **continuing guarantee** and shall remain in full force until all Guaranteed Amount has been irrevocably paid in full.

3.2 This Deed shall not be discharged by:

- Any intermediate payment or settlement of account;
- Any change in the constitution of the Principal Debtor;
- Death, bankruptcy, insolvency, liquidation, judicial management, or receivership of the Principal Debtor;
- Renewal, restructuring, or variation of the Facilities.

3.3 This Deed shall be in addition to and not in substitution for any other security or guarantee held by the Creditor.

---

# ARTICLE 4: INDEMNITY

4.1 As a separate and independent obligation, the Guarantor agrees to indemnify the Creditor on demand against all losses, damages, costs, and expenses suffered or incurred by the Creditor as a result of:

- Any Default;
- Any obligation of the Principal Debtor being void, unenforceable, or illegal;
- Any failure by the Principal Debtor to perform its obligations.

4.2 This indemnity constitutes a **primary obligation** and shall remain enforceable notwithstanding any invalidity of the Principal Agreement.

---

# ARTICLE 5: WAIVERS

5.1 The Guarantor hereby waives any right to require the Creditor to:

- Proceed against the Principal Debtor first;
- Enforce any security;
- Exercise any other remedy before enforcing this Deed.

5.2 The Guarantor waives any defence arising from:

- Variation of the Principal Agreement;
- Grant of time or indulgence;
- Release or compromise with the Principal Debtor;
- Failure to perfect or enforce any security.

5.3 The Guarantor agrees that Sections 133, 134, 135, 139 and 141 of the Contracts Act 1950 shall not operate to discharge the Guarantor to the fullest extent permitted by law.

---

# ARTICLE 6: RIGHTS OF GUARANTOR

6.1 The Guarantor shall not exercise any right of subrogation, contribution, reimbursement, or indemnity against the Principal Debtor unless and until the Guaranteed Amount has been fully paid.

6.2 Upon full payment, the Guarantor shall be entitled to rights under Sections 93 and 94 of the Contracts Act 1950.

---

# ARTICLE 7: REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

The Guarantor represents and warrants that:

7.1 He/She has read and fully understood this Deed.

7.2 He/She has been advised to obtain independent legal advice.

7.3 He/She is not an undischarged bankrupt.

7.4 Execution of this Deed does not contravene any law or court order.

7.5 This Deed constitutes legal, valid and binding obligations enforceable under Malaysian law.

---

# ARTICLE 8: COVENANTS

8.1 Until full settlement of the Guaranteed Amount, the Guarantor shall not:

- Dispose of substantial assets;
- Create encumbrances over assets materially affecting ability to repay;
- Take steps that may prejudice the Creditor’s rights.

---

# ARTICLE 9: NOTICES

9.1 Any notice shall be in writing and deemed served:

- If delivered by hand – upon delivery;
- If sent by registered post – three (3) business days after posting;
- If sent electronically – upon transmission confirmation.

---

# ARTICLE 10: GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

10.1 This Deed shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Malaysia.

10.2 The parties irrevocably submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Courts of Malaysia.

---

---

# ARTICLE 12: SEVERABILITY

12.1 If any provision is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.
