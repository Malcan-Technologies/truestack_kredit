# Loan Calculator

The Loan Calculator is a powerful tool designed to help you quickly generate accurate loan repayment quotes for prospective customers. Calculate monthly payments, view fee breakdowns, and share professional quotes via WhatsApp — all without creating an application.

---

## Why Use the Loan Calculator?

- **Quick Quotes** — Generate instant loan calculations for customer inquiries
- **Product Comparison** — Compare different loan products side by side
- **WhatsApp Ready** — Copy formatted quotes directly to WhatsApp
- **No Commitment** — Calculate without creating applications or borrower records
- **Accurate Calculations** — Uses the same formulas as actual loan processing

---

## Accessing the Calculator

Navigate to **Dashboard → Tools → Calculator** from the sidebar.

---

## Using the Calculator

### Step 1: Filter Products (Optional)

The filter bar helps you quickly find the right product for your customer. Two filters are available:

#### Borrower Type Filter

| Option | Shows Products For |
|--------|-------------------|
| **All** | All borrower types |
| **Individual** | Personal loans for individual borrowers |
| **Corporate** | Business loans for companies (SSM registered) |

#### Schedule Type Filter

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **All** | All schedule types |
| **Jadual J** | Loans without collateral (unsecured) |
| **Jadual K** | Loans with collateral (secured) |

Click any filter button to apply it. Active filters are highlighted. If no products match your filters, click **"Clear Filters"** to reset.

---

### Step 2: Select a Loan Product

Browse the available products and click on one to select it. Each product card displays:

| Information | What It Means |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Product Name** | The loan product's name (e.g., "Personal Loan - Flat Rate") |
| **Interest Model Badge** | How interest is calculated — Flat Rate or Rule 78 |
| **Schedule Type Badge** | Jadual J (unsecured) or Jadual K (secured) |
| **Interest Rate** | Annual interest rate (e.g., 8% p.a.) |
| **Term Range** | Allowed repayment period (e.g., 6-60 months) |
| **Borrower Eligibility** | Who can apply — Individual, Corporate, or All borrowers |

The selected product is highlighted with a blue border and checkmark icon.

---

### Step 3: Enter Loan Details

After selecting a product, enter the customer's requirements:

| Field | Description | Validation |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| **Loan Amount (RM)** | The amount the customer wants to borrow | Must be within the product's min/max limits |
| **Term (months)** | How long to repay the loan | Must be within the product's allowed range |

The allowed range is displayed below each field. If you enter a value outside the range, an error message appears in red.

---

### Step 4: Review the Quote

The calculation results appear in a summary table:

#### Loan Details

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Loan Amount** | The principal amount |
| **Loan Term** | Repayment period in months |
| **Interest Rate** | Annual rate with interest model indicator |

#### Fees & Disbursement

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Legal Fee** | Processing fee (shown in grey) |
| **Stamping Fee** | Government duty fee (shown in grey) |
| **Total Fees** | Sum of all fees (shown in amber/orange) |
| **Net Disbursement** | What the customer actually receives after fees (shown in green) |

#### Repayment Summary

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Total Interest** | Total interest charged over the loan term |
| **Total Payable** | Total amount to be repaid (principal + interest) |
| **Monthly Repayment** | Fixed monthly payment amount (prominently highlighted) |

## Sharing Quotes via WhatsApp

The **"Copy for WhatsApp"** button creates a professionally formatted message ready to paste into WhatsApp.

### What Gets Copied

The quote includes only the essential information customers need:

- Product name
- Loan Amount
- Loan Term
- Interest Rate (with calculation method)
- Monthly Repayment

**Note:** Fees and net disbursement are intentionally excluded to keep the customer message simple. These details can be discussed when the customer is ready to proceed.

Loan Term: 12 months
Interest Rate: 8% p.a. (Flat)

_Quote generated by TrueKredit_

1. Calculate the loan as described above
2. Click **"Copy for WhatsApp"** — the button shows "Copied" when successful
3. Open WhatsApp and paste into any chat
4. The formatting (bold, italic) will display correctly in WhatsApp

## Understanding Interest Calculations

### Flat Rate Interest

With **Flat Rate**, interest is calculated on the full principal for the entire term, regardless of payments made. The same amount of interest is allocated to each month.

**Formula:**

```
Total Interest = Principal × Annual Rate × (Term ÷ 12)
Monthly Payment = (Principal + Total Interest) ÷ Term
```

**Example:**
- Loan Amount: RM 10,000
- Interest Rate: 8% per annum
- Term: 12 months

```
Total Interest = RM 10,000 × 8% × 1 year = RM 800
Total Payable = RM 10,000 + RM 800 = RM 10,800
Monthly Payment = RM 10,800 ÷ 12 = RM 900
```

---

### Rule 78 Interest

With **Rule 78** (sum-of-digits), total interest is calculated the same way as Flat Rate, but it is **front-loaded** across installments. Early payments have a larger interest portion; later payments have a smaller interest portion. The monthly payment amount and total repayment are the same as Flat Rate.

**Key points:**
- Total interest = Principal × Annual Rate × (Term ÷ 12) — same as Flat Rate
- Monthly payment = (Principal + Total Interest) ÷ Term — same as Flat Rate
- The split between principal and interest in each payment varies: more interest early, more principal late

**Example:**
- Loan Amount: RM 10,000
- Interest Rate: 8% per annum
- Term: 12 months

```
Total Interest = RM 800 (same as Flat Rate)
Monthly Payment = RM 900 (same as Flat Rate)
Total Repayment = RM 10,800
```

The repayment schedule will show higher interest in the first few months and lower interest in the final months, while the total amount repaid remains the same.

## Understanding Fees

### How Fees Work

Fees are configured per product and can be either:

| Type | How It's Calculated | Example |
|------|--------------------| --------|
| **Fixed Amount** | Exact RM value | RM 100 legal fee |
| **Percentage** | % of loan amount | 1% of RM 10,000 = RM 100 |

### Net Disbursement

Fees are deducted from the loan amount **before** disbursement. The customer receives the **Net Disbursement** amount.

**Example:**
- Loan Amount: RM 10,000
- Legal Fee: RM 100
- Stamping Fee: RM 50
- Total Fees: RM 150
- **Net Disbursement: RM 9,850** (amount transferred to customer)

**Important:** The customer repays the full loan amount (RM 10,000 + interest), not the net disbursement.

## Best Practices

### When Talking to Customers

1. **Start with their budget** — Ask what monthly payment they can afford, then work backwards to find the right amount and term.

2. **Explain net disbursement** — Make sure customers understand that fees reduce the amount they receive.

3. **Compare options** — Use filters to quickly show products suitable for their borrower type.

4. **Use WhatsApp sharing** — Send quotes immediately while talking to customers so they have a reference.

### Getting Accurate Quotes

1. **Check product eligibility** — Use the borrower type filter to ensure you're showing appropriate products.

2. **Verify limits** — Each product has different min/max amounts and terms — stay within bounds.

3. **Consider all costs** — Remember that fees affect the net disbursement.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I save quotes for later?

The calculator doesn't save quotes. Use the **"Copy for WhatsApp"** feature to save quotes as messages, or take screenshots for your records.

### Why don't I see any products?

- Check that you have active products configured in **Dashboard → Products**
- Clear any filters that might be hiding products
- Inactive products don't appear in the calculator

### How accurate are the calculations?

The calculator uses the same formulas as the loan processing system. However, these are estimates — actual terms are determined during application review and may vary.

### Can I create a loan application from here?

No, the calculator is for quotes only. To create an application, go to **Dashboard → Applications → New Application** and follow the application workflow.

### Why are fees not included in the WhatsApp quote?

The WhatsApp quote is designed to be simple and customer-friendly. Fees and detailed breakdowns can be discussed when the customer is ready to proceed with an application.
