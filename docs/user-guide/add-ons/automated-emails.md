# Automated Emails Add-on

TrueKredit already generates all loan-related PDFs automatically — receipts, reminder letters, default notices, and discharge letters. The **Automated Emails** add-on takes it a step further by sending these documents directly to borrowers via email, without any manual effort from your team.

---

## Documents Sent Automatically

### Payment Receipts

Sent immediately after each repayment is recorded. The borrower receives a PDF receipt confirming the payment amount, date, and updated loan balance.

### Reminder Letters

Sent before due dates to reduce late payments. Borrowers are reminded of their upcoming repayment schedule, helping improve on-time collection rates.

### Arrears Notice Letters

Triggered when a loan enters the arrears period, or when an admin manually generates an updated arrears letter. The borrower receives the formal notice of overdue repayments, including all outstanding amounts and late fees.

### Default Notice Letters

Sent automatically when a loan is marked as defaulted, or when an admin manually generates an updated default letter. The borrower receives the formal notice of default with the total amount due and consequences.

### Discharge Letters

Sent when a loan is fully settled, completing the borrower lifecycle. The borrower receives a formal confirmation that all obligations have been fulfilled.

---

## Benefits

- **Eliminates manual emailing** — no more downloading PDFs and attaching them to emails
- **Improves borrower communication** — professional, timely correspondence builds trust
- **Reduces late payments** — reminder letters sent before due dates improve collection rates
- **Creates a verifiable digital trail** — all correspondence is recorded and traceable
- **Frees your staff** — your team can focus on higher-value tasks instead of routine email work

---

## Do I Still Get PDFs Without This Add-on?

**Yes.** All PDFs — receipts, reminder letters, arrears notices, default notices, and discharge letters — are generated automatically in TrueKredit regardless of whether you subscribe to this add-on.

The add-on **only automates the sending** of these documents via email. Without it, you can still download and manually send any letter from the loan detail page.

---

## How It Works

1. A loan event occurs (e.g., payment recorded, arrears entered, loan defaulted)
2. TrueKredit generates the corresponding PDF as usual
3. With the add-on enabled, the system automatically sends the PDF to the borrower's email on file
4. The email delivery is logged in the loan's audit trail for your records

No configuration is needed beyond subscribing to the add-on. Emails are sent using the borrower's email address stored in their profile.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What if a borrower doesn't have an email address?

If no email address is on file for the borrower, the letter will still be generated as a PDF but the email will not be sent. You can always download and send it manually.

### Can I disable emails for specific loan events?

Currently, the add-on sends emails for all supported document types. Granular control per event type may be available in a future update.

### Are email deliveries tracked?

Yes. Each email sent is recorded in the loan's audit trail, so you have a complete record of all correspondence.

### How do I subscribe to the Automated Emails add-on?

Contact your TrueKredit account manager or visit the billing section in your admin dashboard to enable the add-on.
