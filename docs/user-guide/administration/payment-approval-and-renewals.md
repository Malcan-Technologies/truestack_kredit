# Payment Approval & Late Renewals

This guide explains how subscription payment approval, overdue renewals, and billing-cycle timing work in TrueKredit.

---

## How Payment Approval Works

For subscription renewals and add-on purchases, payment does not activate instantly on submission.

The usual flow is:

1. You submit payment details or proof of payment.
2. TrueKredit creates a billing request for review.
3. The request is reviewed by an authorized admin.
4. Once approved, your invoice is marked as paid and your subscription or add-on is activated.

If a payment request is still under review, you may see a pending approval status in billing.

---

## What Happens If Payment Is Late

If a renewal invoice is not settled by its due date:

- your account may be marked as **OVERDUE**
- the renewal invoice remains collectible
- the subscription is **not automatically cancelled** just because payment is late

This means you can still settle the renewal after the due date without creating a brand-new billing cycle.

---

## Does A Late Payment Reset My Billing Cycle?

No.

When a late renewal is approved, the billing cycle continues from the original renewal period, not from the later approval date.

Example:

- Your previous cycle ends on `3 Apr`
- Your renewal covers `3 Apr` to `3 May`
- You settle it late on `10 Apr`

Your renewed period still remains `3 Apr` to `3 May`

This ensures the days used while payment was outstanding are still part of the same billed period.

---

## Important Difference Between New Subscription And Renewal

### First subscription

For a brand-new subscription, activation begins when the initial subscription is approved.

### Renewal

For a renewal, approval keeps the original renewal period. It does not move the cycle forward to the approval date.

---

## Add-On Purchases

### TrueSend mid-cycle activation

If you activate TrueSend during an active billing period:

- first-time activation may be prorated for the remaining days in the current cycle
- re-subscribing after a previous cancellation is charged as a full add-on period

### Approval

Add-on purchases also go through payment approval before they become active, unless your organization processes them through a separate manual admin workflow.

---

## Overdue Does Not Mean Cancelled

An overdue status means payment is outstanding.

It does **not** mean your subscription was automatically cancelled.

Cancellation is handled separately and is not triggered automatically just because a renewal is late.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### I submitted payment. Why is my status still pending?

Your payment request may still be waiting for approval. Check the Billing page for the latest request or invoice status.

### My renewal was approved late. Why did the cycle not move forward?

This is expected. Renewals keep their original billing period so the unpaid days are still accounted for correctly.

### If I miss payment, will my subscription be cancelled automatically?

No. Your account may become overdue, but overdue status and cancellation are different.

### Can I still pay after becoming overdue?

Yes. Overdue renewal invoices remain payable and can still go through the approval flow.
