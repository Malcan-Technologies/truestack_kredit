# Getting Started with TrueKredit

Welcome to TrueKredit! This guide will help you get started with managing loans for your organization.

---

## Overview

TrueKredit is a comprehensive loan management platform that helps you:

- **Manage Borrowers** - Keep track of your borrowers' information and loan history
- **Configure Loan Products** - Define different loan types with their own interest rates and terms
- **Process Loan Applications** - Review and approve loan applications
- **Track Repayments** - Monitor loan schedules and record payments
- **Generate Reports** - Get insights into your loan portfolio

---

## Quick Start

### Step 1: Set Up Your Loan Products

Before you can issue loans, you need to configure at least one loan product. A product defines:

- Interest rate and calculation method
- Minimum and maximum loan amounts
- Allowed repayment terms
- Late payment penalties

Go to **Products** in the sidebar to create your first product.

### Step 2: Add Borrowers

Register your borrowers in the system. You'll need:

- Full name
- IC number (unique identifier)
- Contact information (phone, email)
- Address

Go to **Borrowers** in the sidebar to add borrowers.

### Step 3: Create a Loan Application

Once you have products and borrowers:

1. Go to **Applications**
2. Click **New Application**
3. Select a borrower and product
4. Enter the loan amount and term
5. Save as draft or submit for review

### Step 4: Approve and Disburse

After an application is submitted, an **Owner or Admin** must approve it:

1. Review the application details
2. Click **Approve** to create the loan (Owner/Admin only)
3. Click **Disburse** when funds are released
4. The system will generate the repayment schedule

### Step 5: Track Payments

Go to **Loans** and select a loan to:

- View the repayment schedule
- Record payments as they come in
- See payment history and outstanding balance

## User Roles

Your account has one of three roles within each tenant:

| Role | Capabilities |
|------|--------------|
| **Owner** | Full access to all features including billing, team management, and ownership transfer |
| **Admin** | Manage loans, borrowers, products, approve applications, access admin logs and billing |
| **Staff** | Day-to-day loan operations — create borrowers, applications, record payments, access compliance. Cannot approve applications, manage products, or access billing/admin logs |

For a detailed breakdown, see [Roles & Permissions](?doc=getting-started/roles-and-permissions).
